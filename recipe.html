Recipe #1

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Peeled, cooked potatoes 250 g
butter 1 table spoon
Spinache 250 g
large egg 1
lvegetable oil 1 teaspoon
    Steps
  1. Cook peeled potatoes until they start falling of a tooth pick
  2. Mix potatoes with butter and stomp with masher
  3. Cook spinache in 1 liter of water until creamy
  4. Fry 1 large egg
  5. Put mashed potatoes on plate
  6. Add spinache, partially covering the mashed potatoes
  7. Crown the mashed potatoes with the fried egg
  8. Enjoy

Recipe #2

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
vegetable oil 1 teaspoon
potatoes 250 g
Tomatoes 150 g
eggs 1 - 2 large eggs
    Steps
  1. Heat frying pan until heat can be felt with open hand 10 cm off the pan/li>
  2. Slice potatoes and add to frying pan
  3. Do the same with tomatoes
  4. When potatoes and tomatoes are cooked (tooth pick test), add eggs and stir the content
  5. Serve on a plate and enjuoy

Recipe #3

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Instant beef stock 5.5 g
Mixed vegetables 170 g - 255 g
Spaghetti 85 g
    Steps
  1. Add ingredients to cooking pot
  2. Add about 1 liter of water
  3. Cook until noodles are done
  4. Enjoy 2 - 3 bowls
